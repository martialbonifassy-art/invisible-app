Wood objects & digital whispers

The programmed emotion of matter.

Wooden jewels and objects equipped with NFC that whisper unique messages written and spoken by a poetic AI.
Each piece is crafted in the workshop, then connected to an artificial intelligence that composes intimate messages from just a few details: a first name, a memory, a date, a place, an intention. Every time the jewel is scanned, it breathes a new whisper — until its messages are exhausted, and can be recharged at the workshop.

Simply scan the NFC chip with a compatible smartphone to hear the voice of the wood.
1. The object

A jewel or wooden object, crafted by hand with an NFC chip discreetly embedded.

2. Configuration

You choose who it is for, what you wish it to whisper, and a few personal details.

3. The living whisper

The AI composes and speaks a unique message each time the jewel is opened, like a secret entrusted to the wood.
