Wooden objects & digital whispers

The emotion programmed into matter.

Wooden jewels and objects, equipped with an NFC chip, whispering unique messages written and read by a poetic AI.
Each piece is crafted in the workshop, then linked to an artificial intelligence that composes intimate messages from a few details: a first name, a memory, a date, a place, an intention. Each time the phone comes close, the jewel breathes a new whisper – until its messages are exhausted, then can be recharged at the workshop.

1. The object

A jewel or wooden object, handcrafted, with a discreetly embedded NFC chip.

2. The personalization

You choose who it is for, what you want it to whisper, and a few personal details.

3. The living whisper

The AI voice composes and reads a unique message each time it's opened, like a secret entrusted to the wood.
