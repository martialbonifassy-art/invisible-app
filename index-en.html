Wooden objects & digital whispers

The emotion programmed into matter.

Wooden jewellery and objects, equipped with an NFC chip, that whisper unique messages written by a poetic AI.
Each piece is crafted in the studio, then connected to an artificial intelligence that composes intimate messages, based on a first name, a memory, a date or a place. Each time the phone approaches the jewel, it releases a new whisper – until its 100 messages are used, and can be recharged on request.

Simply scan the NFC chip with a compatible smartphone to hear the whisper of the day.
1. The object

A wooden jewel or object, hand–crafted, with a discreet NFC chip inside.

2. The personalization

You choose a first name, an intention, a few details. The AI weaves from these elements.

3. The whisper

Each time it meets a phone, a new intimate message is generated, up to 100 whispers.
