Wooden objects & digital whispers

The programmed emotion of matter.

Handcrafted wooden jewels and objects, equipped with an NFC chip, that whisper unique messages written and spoken by a poetic AI.
Each piece is created in the workshop, then linked to an artificial intelligence that composes intimate messages from a few details: a first name, a memory, a date, a place, an intention. Each time a phone touches the jewel, it releases a new whisper – until its quota of messages is reached, then rechargeable at the workshop.

Simply scan the NFC chip with a compatible smartphone to hear the voice of the wood.
1. The object

A jewel or wooden object, handcrafted, with a discreet NFC chip inside.

2. Personalisation

You choose who it is for, what you want it to whisper, and a few personal details.

3. The living whisper

The AI voice composes and reads a unique message each time it is opened, like a secret entrusted to the wood.

Example whisper
“Marie, like that spring in Toulouse, know that every step on this new path is accompanied by a soft light you cannot see yet, but the wood has already recognised.”
